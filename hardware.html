Hardware

InferASIC is focused on purpose-built inference cards for server deployment. Hardware is under design.

Design Direction

The target form factor is card-based accelerators intended for standard server deployment. The design direction emphasizes transformer inference with a current focus on independent card execution: each card is designed for self-contained execution, and the model can be updated (the hardware is not locked to fixed silicon). System scale is presently being explored through deployment of many cards rather than distributed execution across cards. The goal is efficient, purpose-built inference in standard infrastructure. Technical details remain under development and are not disclosed publicly.

Long-Term Product Vision

Over the long term, the vision is inference cards that slot into standard servers and can be reprogrammed as models evolve. How that maps to specific products, SKUs, or timelines is part of ongoing design work. No product announcements or availability dates exist at this stage.

Current stage. Any hardware described here is conceptual and under design. No physical hardware exists yet. Specifications and plans are subject to change as the project develops.
